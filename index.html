I am a geologist turned software engineer. My dabbling in technology during my environmental consulting career ultimately led me to enroll in the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy. I love creating new apps using both frontend and backend technologies with a focus on the NERDS stack (Node, Express, React/Redux, Databases with SQL).

When I'm not coding, you can find me walking the parks, catching up on my Steam library backlog, or picking out a tune on the piano.
