About

My name is Kathleen McDaniel and I am a geologist turned software engineer. My dabbling in technology during my environmental consulting career had me checking out resources like freeCodeCamp, the University of Helsinki's Java MOOC, and ultimately led me to enroll in the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy. I love creating new apps using both frontend and backend technologies with a focus on the NERDS stack (Node, Express, React/Redux, Databases with SQL).
